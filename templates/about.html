about us.

TigerRides is a student run app created to streamline the process of students sharing Uber/Lyft/other transportation services to and from the Princeton University campus. This will reduce transportation costs by allowing for easier communication between traveling students. Essentially, we hope to create a ride-share-share application for Princeton students.

{% load static %}

Simply add your location, date, and time requirements and some info about your preferences, and we will help you find students to split a ride with! You can join or leave a ride as needed, and see other students' profile information to easily connect and communicate. You can also create your profile quickly with your TigerBook information and edit it as preferred. Lastly, we implement a 5-star rating system in order to encourage accountability for all participating riders when it comes to communication, promptness, and responsibility with splitting payments.
